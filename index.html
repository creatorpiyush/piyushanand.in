README preview · rendered

I'm a software engineer at Nagarro, where I've spent the last four years growing from Trainee to Associate Staff Engineer - mostly by chasing problems that sit at the edge of what I already know how to do.

These days that means leading AI chapter development for my business unit: architecting RAG systems on Azure OpenAI and FlowiseAI, and running workshops to help other engineers actually ship with them. Before that, I spent a good stretch building a Flutter loyalty app for a German airline used by more than 5 million people, and automating the CI/CD pipelines that got our releases out faster and with less manual QA.

I like systems that are both correct and pleasant to operate - pipelines that don't page anyone at 2am, mobile apps that feel instant, and AI features that fail gracefully instead of confidently making things up. Outside of client work, I've published research on ML-based COVID-19 detection, open-sourced raglite-toolkit as both an npm and a PyPI package, and I keep a running list of side projects, from offline LLM apps to real-time multiplayer games.
